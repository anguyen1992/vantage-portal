Quarterly Look-Through Analysis

As at: 31 Mar 2026 Last updated: 11 May 2026 Owner: Anthony Nguyen Source: HUB24 / Underlying Fund Reports
Methodology — Look-through positions aggregate the underlying holdings of each SMA constituent — direct equity positions plus the top reported holdings of each managed fund and ETF — weighted by their share of the SMA. Benchmarks: VAS (S&P/ASX 300) for Australian Equities; VGS (MSCI World ex-Australia) for Global Equities. Stock-level totals reflect top reported holdings and may understate the true portfolio weight where underlying fund disclosure is limited to top 10. Sector and regional figures are computed from full sector/regional breakdowns disclosed by each underlying fund.
